Refund Policy

We understand that plans can change, and we strive to provide a fair and straightforward refund policy for our customers. Please review the following guidelines regarding cancellations and refunds.

1.Cancellations Within 15 Minutes:

If you cancel your booking within 15 minutes of making the reservation, you are eligible for a full refund (100% of the booking amount).

2.Cancellations Within 30 Minutes.

If you cancel your booking within 30 minutes of making the reservation, you are eligible for a partial refund (50% of the booking amount).

3.Cancellations After 30 Minutes.

If you cancel your booking after 30 minutes of making the reservation, no refund will be issued.

Please ensure to cancel your booking within the specified time frames to be eligible for a refund. If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our customer support team.
